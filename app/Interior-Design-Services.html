Our Process

Marrying aesthetics and functionality, Skywide services the Philadelphia area in Pennsylvania to design beautiful and practical spaces. Our team of the best interior designers and interior decorators experienced in project management believes in a holistic approach to interior design, where every element complements the other.

Client Briefing & Ideation

Choosing the interior design for a space is highly dependent on one’s style, preferences, and layout of the space. With a wide variety of styles like minimalist, contemporary, modern, and more, our process begins with a meeting for you to share your goals, inspiration, and budget to help us design a space you love.

Design Mockups & 3D Visualization

Next, our team prepares a design mockup or 3D visualization to help you imagine your space better. This helps with space planning such as determining electrical outlet locations, placement of built in furniture, and how it works with the functionality and flow of the room, saving time and ensuring that the project stays within budget.

Material & Decor Selection

Our team also takes additional time to finalize the material and décor selection you desire for your space. These elements that accentuate the overall design, add functionality, and cover up structural components determine how the design comes together so it feels coherent and complete.

Full-Service Installation

Installation Day can be both exciting and overwhelming, the final stage of turning dreams into reality. Despite the organized chaos, our installation team with years of experience ensures that everything is well planned and executed, right down to unrolling the area rug, ready for your arrival.

Final Reveal

Any interior design project is both an emotional and financial investment. After the final reveal, our team follows up to ensure that all the changes are to your satisfaction.
