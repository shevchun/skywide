Our Process

Home renovation is personal. If you are already living in your dream home in the Philadelphia area, Skywide’s full services also include home additions and home repairs. With the uniqueness of each home in mind, we do an excellent job at capturing the essence of the residents and the home’s history. 

Meeting & Home Evaluation

Our process begins with a meeting and home evaluation, understanding your needs, preferences, and the space involved. Sitting down with our team to help us understand your vision is immensely helpful in making your dreams a reality.

Conceptual Design

After the meeting and evaluation, we then provide a conceptual design to ensure it all flows seamlessly and functions better to fit your needs. This step also helps you to envision the remodeled space better.

Material Selection

Material selection is a crucial step as it affects the budget and determines how the entire design comes together to feel complete and coherent. For example, the materials needed for a master bath or kitchen remodeling project would be vastly different. 

Renovation & Remodeling

After reaching an agreement on the conceptual design and materials to be used in the remodeling project , our team of general contractors, handymen, craftsmen, and electricians get to work to bring your vision to life.

Quality Check & Handover

Once completed, there is the final reveal, quality check, and handover. Our team stays ready and follows up regularly to ensure that you are satisfied with the aesthetics, flow, and functionality of the design.
