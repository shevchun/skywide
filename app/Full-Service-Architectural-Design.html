Our Process

From interior design services to customized new constructions, our architectural designs are a blend of innovation, sustainability, and local sensibilities. Our commercial and residential projects consider the environment, client's vision, and the latest architectural trends while serving those who utilize the space involved.

Initial Client Consultation

Our process begins with an initial meeting between you and our team to ensure that we understand your goals, specifications, preferences, and budget. This step also includes going through the documents, permits, and more.

Preliminary Design

Next, our team works with you to understand the site and structures nearby, ideas and concepts for the space, local zoning restrictions, scale, building codes, project scope, and more before coming up with a preliminary design.

Schematic Design

The pre-designs are then developed into a proposal to be presented to you. It will include the ideas from pre-design along with site and floor plans, structural, electrical, plumbing systems, and more. The schematic design is updated regularly based on your feedback.

Design Development

After agreeing on the schematic design, we start creating a more detailed plan that includes window and door placements, interior and exterior finishes, and more. This stage finalizes the layout, dimensions, exterior, and materials used with very minor changes going forwards.

Construction Documents

Working construction documents are used by a contractor for pricing, bidding, and construction. The building permit set is delivered to the permitting authority for approval and the construction set with design details and dimensions are for the builders.

Building Permits

The city or county will then review the building permit based on zoning laws, building codes, and structure. Approval is required before construction commences. Approval time varies depending on the size and type of project, along with specific factors such as being a historical building.

Bidding & Negotiation

We have our own building team, but if you would like to negotiate with another contractor, we assist in finding the best fit in terms of qualifications and costs by helping with the bidding process, analyzing proposals, and bid award.

Construction Administration

In this final phase, our architect visits your construction site regularly to deal with questions and issues that may arise. This helps to avoid any costly delays. At this point, the construction team is in-charge and this phase lasts throughout until the construction is completed.
