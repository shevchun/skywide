Our Process

Our construction management strategy prioritizes effective communication, meticulous planning, and rigorous monitoring to ensure that building projects run smoothly with the help of specialized techniques and software.

Project Planning & Initiation

We begin with project planning - a process that includes understanding the needs and goals of the building, determining the schematic design and developing it, considering the preconstruction schedule and contract documents involved, and ensuring that the build meets building codes and regulations.

Resource Allocation

When allocating resources, we consider the estimated construction costs, including labor, equipment, and materials, along with soft costs. This is an integral part of cost management which involves making partial payments to general contractors and subcontractors for work completed as the project progresses.

On-site Project Management

Besides managing on-site logistics involving elements such as employee or visitor safety, parking, and construction traffic, our team also works to foster a positive environment that encourages collaboration, effective communication, and facilitating the approvals needed to keep the project going.

Project Closure & Quality Assurance

Once the project is completed, occupancy of the space begins and a warranty period begins. During this stage, our team helps with project closure and quality assurance which includes ensuring that all the quality, materials, and equipment are according to expectations as stated within the terms of the contract.
