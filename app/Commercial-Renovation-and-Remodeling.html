Our Process

Our approach to commercial remodeling aligns aesthetics with functionality to achieve a balance between form and function. Backed with an experienced team in high quality commercial construction and project management, we cater to both existing and new constructions.

Initial Consultation

Each commercial project is unique. To help you achieve your goals and preferences in terms of design, our entire team sits in during the initial consultation to give you the time and attention you deserve. Done right, these projects deliver high ROI while improving property value.

Site Assessment

Site assessment includes going through specifications such as the Certificate of Occupancy; accessing building permits and records; reviewing potential structural, electrical, plumbing issues; and more. This gives us an idea of what needs to be done and how best to do it.

Design and Planning

Considering your goals and the site, our team starts planning the design and materials to be used, ensuring that the remodeled or renovated space flows well, is aesthetically pleasing, and improves functionality. Using 3D visualization, you will be able to imagine the outcome better.

Execution

After agreeing on the design and materials to be used, our comprehensive building team of experienced general contractors and subcontractors who function well as a unit starts remodeling or renovating the space to ensure you meet your goals and deadline.

Final Walkthrough and Handover

After everything has been completed according to plan, we go through a final reveal to discuss the results and to see if there are any finishing touches required before the handover. Our team diligently follows up with all our clients to make sure that there are no issues or concerns.
